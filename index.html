About this page

  • What our mission is.

    • This database's goal is to provide industry and community growth in Arkansas film making industry. Our hope is that newly up and coming film crews, directors, and artists gain inspiration and ideas from using our database of movies produced in Arkansas.

  • What we hope to accomplish

    • Some of our movies here in Arkansas were filmed in hisotrical and beautiful locations such as "The Old Mill" or "Christ of the Ozarks" or the legendary baseball stadium of "Lamar Porter". This database hopes to accomplish the use of these beautiful locations in future film ideas

  • The Old Mill
  • Christ of The Ozarks
  • Lamar Porter baseball field
  • How this idea came about

    • People like myself and others that are passionate about the film making industry thought of a way to bring our extreme passion to light and we needed an outlet, thus the Database was born.

  • Looking ahead into the future

    • The Arkansas Moive Database would love for its site to act as a commonwealth for more inspuring film industry enthusiasts in the future, or everyday people just wanting to look up their favorite movies of the decade or genre.
